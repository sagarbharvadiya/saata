Operations Team update
Dear SAATA members,

Migration of current membership data to Zoho completed successfully! We had some teething issues but with support from Manish Narayan and Nisha George from the Operations team and Harshal Pradhan, our technical support partner, we were able to do this quite effectively and on time. We continue to streamline the process over next few months and will also be migrating the data of previous members so that we consolidate all our member data on Zoho. We invite all members who have not yet created their Zoho accounts to do so to renew memberships and access membership details.

The Operations team worked with BOT to finalize the SAATA Grievance Redressal Procedure and SAATA Professional Practices and Ethics Guidelines. The documents are uploaded on SAATA website under Governance tab and we encourage all members to go through them @ https://www.saata.org/governance/

Malarvizhi Kasi, one of our TA trainees from Chennai, joined Operations Team from April 2023 to support us in the Zoho platform and membership subscription management. Welcome Malar!
